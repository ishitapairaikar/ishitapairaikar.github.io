AI Governance Consulting

I help organizations answer two questions:
Are we building the right AI, and are we building AI right?

Through structured workshops, I help teams identify stakeholders, map potential impacts and risks, determine which governance principles matter most for a specific use case, and establish clear accountability through RACI frameworks.

For organizations deploying or procuring AI, I develop governance blueprints: requirements, audit processes, escalation pathways, disclosure standards, and vendor accountability mechanisms. Recent focus: healthcare and public sector AI.

Approach informed by mentorship from Phaedra Boinodiris, IBM Consulting's Responsible AI Leader.
